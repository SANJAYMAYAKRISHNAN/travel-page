About Bali

Bali, Indonesia's most famous island, is renowned for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs. The island is home to religious sites such as the cliffside Uluwatu Temple. The beachside city of Kuta has lively bars, while Seminyak, Sanur, and Nusa Dua are popular resort towns.
